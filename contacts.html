Resources

Here we listed a couple of non-profit organizations and programs that can help you through this time.

Non-profit Organizations

  1. Fristers

    2. Fristers provides the community of support that young parents need to thrive. They provide education, access to resources, role models, and support to young parents and their children. Fristers invests in lives, families and generations to come.

    Fristers, founded and operated on Christian principles, is dedicated to helping teen parents build healthy and stable lives and families through educational classes and workshops, mentoring, case management, and the support of a caring community.

    Fristers offers multiples solutions to the issue:

    • A community of support improves teen parents' mental health and well-being
    • Referrals and resources to complete high school
    • Introduction to college and vocational training
    • Life skills and workforce readiness guide young parents toward economic security
    • Healthy relationships and healthy parenting training create a thriving family environment.

    Children receive direct services that promote healthy development and school readiness.

  2. Teen Parent Connection

    3. Teen Parent Connection is a nonprofit agency serving teen parents in DuPage County as they navigate the challenges of both parenthood and adolescence. In addition, we offer school-based pregnancy prevention education programs in both middle schools and high schools to help reduce the risk of teen pregnancy.

    This organization is a staff, board, and community dedicated to empowering young families and creating the best possible start for young mothers, young fathers, and their babies.

    Teen Parent Connection is the only nonprofit, organization in DuPage County exclusively focused on providing comprehensive programs and services related to teenage pregnancy and parenting.

    Mission: To serve the community through education on the realities and responsibilities of teenage pregnancy, and through long-term assistance to adolescent parents for their development of self-esteem, parenting skills, and empowerment toward self-sufficiency.

  3. Breath of Life

    4. Each client receives pregnancy and postpartum support from caring, dedicated volunteers and staff. Young mothers can access training courses, workshops, counseling, mentor sessions, and support from other teen mothers. The Center also offers parenting classes, as well as free materials such as clothing, diapers, and formula.

    When an expectant mom makes a decision to have her baby, we are committed to extending help to her during her greatest time of need! Breath Of Life's goal is to help our expectant moms make informed choices through education and encouragement. The Breath of Life Pregnancy Center is a safe place where women with no where to turn can find comfort, direction and care in times of an unplanned pregnancy.

  4. The Northwest Center

    5. Mission: The Northwest Center seeks to promote the dignity of women and a respect for all human life.

    We seek to accomplish this goal by offering the loving support and comprehensive aid necessary to enable all women to continue their pregnancies, deliver healthy babies, and adequately care for themselves and their children.

  5. Generation Her

    6. Generation Her is a non-profit organization operating in Southern California, that seeks to empower teenage mothers, ages 13-25, and their children by connecting them to a supportive community of other teen mothers and adult mentors, equipping them with desired life skills, and, ultimately, impacting their future in a positive way."

    Generation Her is a non-profit organization that was founded in 2009. Generation Her encourages setting educational and career goals for each teen mom that we work with and walks beside her to accomplish her goals as well as to provide a safe place of encouragement, hope and support through mentors and their fellow community of teenage mothers.

    Generation Her offers:

    • Child safety training
    • Promoting and rewarding completion of goals
    • Workshops on: relationships, goals, finances, anger, sex education, money management, etc.
    • One-on-one mentor relationships
    • Rewarding positive parenting practices with baby clothes and other baby needs
    • Regular celebrations of annual holidays & milestones
    • Access to parent & child resources
    • Promotion of healthy parenting
    • Participation in creative projects
    • Life skills training

Free Programs

  1. Teen Success

    2. ​​Teen Success, Inc. empowers young mothers to graduate high school, pursue post-secondary education, and break out of the cycle of poverty for themselves and their children.

    Our participants work to complete high school or its equivalent, and develop strong parenting skills to nurture their child's development.

  2. Child Development Council: Parenting Program (TP3)

    3. Parenting can be challenging and nobody is born knowing how to parent. If you are a new teen mom or dad, you probably have many questions or are not sure where to turn for help. At TP3, we can help you find the resources and parenting strategies you need to help you be the best parent you can be.

    TP3 offers:

    • One-on-one attention with a personal case manager
    • Pre-natal, parenting and child development information
    • Personal goal setting
    • Information and referrals to appropriate agencies
    • Help with applying to assistance programs
    • Help with educational and employment goals
    • Help with educational and employment goals
    • Children’s clothing and equipment, child safety tips, and finding childcare
    • General advocacy

    At TP3, we adhere to strict confidentiality guidelines and work out of a strength-based model. We believe you are the expert on your own life. The voluntary program serves youth up to age 22.

There are many organizations and programs to help you, as a teen, parent a child and complete your life goals. These are only a few of them. We encourage you to talk to a health care professional to help you find the best organization or program near you! We wish the best of luck during this journey.
